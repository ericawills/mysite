About Me

I’ve been a software developer for over 5 years, between college courses, Full Stack bootcamp, and freelance projects.

I’ve mostly worked with Javascript, Node.JS, and Python but also a little bit with Java, PHP, and C.

I am currently working on creating a blog where I can post intriguing developer content, dish about new technologies, and share what I know with others who are passionate about software development too.

I like building new stuff and working with other people – nothing really exciting is ever built alone!

  • My Interests:
  • Software architecture
  • MEAN Stack
  • Learning new programming languages & technologies
  • Modern art
  • Writing

My work philosophies:

  • Clarity is key! Nothing is better or more high quality than the result of a well-planned, clearly-documented, and concisely-formatted project.
  • Learn something new – whether it be from an accomplishment, a new task, or even a mistake, Learn from it!

Beside programming I am interested in art, culture, and cuisine.

I am a foodie that loves to travel and try new foods. Zagat and Yelp are my go to’s for trying new hotspots.

I am also very interested in painting. Working with acrylic on canvas is a pastime of mine and when I need inspiration, the art museums and D.C are extremely therapeutic.
